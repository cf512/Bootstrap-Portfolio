About Me

I am a lifelong Texan, was born in Houston in 1982, have been in Austin since 1992, went to Texas A&M University (Fightin' Texas Aggie Class of '06), got married in 2012 (to the love of my life, Jessica Finnigan), have an infant daughter (our sweet baby girl, Haley Finnigan), enjoy fishing, the outdoors, and traveling to see concerts.

Full Stack Web Developer educated at the University of Texas at Austin with strong skills in JavaScript, jQuery, CSS, and ReactJS. Favorite aspects of software development are solving complex problems with code, writing tests to ensure quality, working in groups, and conceptualizing and creating useful, fun websites. Prior experience and expertise in managed web hosting support and WordPress. Currently a paid contractor for the open-source CurlConverter tool, used by over 14,000 unique visitors monthly. Have gained exposure through this project to other languages like Dart, Go, PHP, Python, R, and Rust. Started using computers in the early 80s on a Macintosh 512K at the age of 3, and therefore have a unique perspective into the technologies and personalities of Generations X & Y. In the near-term, I am pursuing an associate or junior level developer role. In the long-term, I plan to pursue engineering leadership roles.
