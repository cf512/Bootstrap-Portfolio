About Me

I am a lifelong Texan, was born in Houston in 1982, have been in Austin since 1992, went to Texas A&M University (Fightin' Texas Aggie Class of '06), got married in 2012 (to the love of my life, Jessica Finnigan), have an infant daughter (our sweet baby girl, Haley Finnigan), enjoy fishing, the outdoors, and traveling to see concerts.

Professionally, I have strong skills in JavaScript, jQuery, AJAX, CSS, HTML5, SQL, and experience with PHP and Laravel. Previously I was a Senior Support Engineer with WP Engine for almost 4 years and have expertise with WordPress. Regarding the job description’s “a plus, but not required” items, I actually do have a lot of experience with graphic design and audio/video editing and production. Additionally, I have 10+ years experience with the nonprofit industry, as a volunteer board member for the Austin Sunshine Camps and Young Men’s Business League, where I still currently oversee all technical operations.
